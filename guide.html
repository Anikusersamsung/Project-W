1. How Invoicing Works

The Invoice Maker is designed to be flexible for both quick sales and formal business transactions. There are two modes:

Customer Receipt (Simple)

Best for walk-in customers. It generates a simple list of items, total cost, and date. No complex header information is required.

You can generate a receipt when invoice is paid. Customize the receipt in app settings there are full options to customize a customer receipt.

Business Invoice (B2B)

Best for selling to other shops. You can add:

  • Title: Title can be Default or Your Shop name.
  • Business Header/Name: Your Shop Name or Your name, Phone, and Address (set in drawer).
  • Customer Info: The buyer's Name and Contact info.
  • Invoice ID: Auto-generated unique number or custom number for tracking.
Note: For knowing the business invoice, the system auto generate this "BusInv-" in the invoice number.

2. Product Listing

Adding products is the core of the app. To add a product, tap the + button on the home/product screen.

  • Name: Give a name of the item.
  • Price: Your selling price.
  • Cost (Optional): What you paid for it (calculated for profit reports).
  • Category: Assign a category label to organize your products.
  • Barcode: Scan or type the code manually. Upc code help to find products instantly.
Note: Scanner can make mistake to get Upc/Barcode, *recommend you to check the code after scanning.

How to Find Products

You can find products in two ways:
1. Search Bar: Type the name, code or product id.
2. Scan Button: Tap the Scanner icon and point it at a barcode. The app will instantly jump to that product if code exist.

3. Category Logic

Categories help you filter products (e.g., Electronics, Food, Clothing). You can rename it.

⚠️ Why can't I delete a Category?
You can only delete empty categories. If a category has products assigned to it, you can't delete it. Beacause the app needs to keep data integrity and avoid orphaned products without a category. 

        Solution: First, delete the assigned products inside the category (or move them to another
        category), and then you can delete the category itself.

 5. Product Variations

      
Use variations when you have one product (e.g., "T-Shirt") that comes in different versions (e.g., "Red",
        "Blue", "Size XL").

      
Instead of creating "T-Shirt Red" and "T-Shirt Blue" as separate items, create one "T-Shirt" and add variations
        inside it. Each variation can have its own name and barcode.

 7. Backup & Import

      
Since the app is offline, if you lose your phone, you lose your data unless you backup.

      
    
        
  • Download Backup: Generates a `.bin` file containing your entire database. Save this to
          Google Drive or your PC.
    • 
        
  • Import: Select a previously saved `.bin` file to restore your app data.
    • 
      

      

        Warning: Don't forget to backup regularly! We cannot recover lost data if you lose your device
        or uninstall the app without a backup.
      

    


    

      
 8. Technical Features


      
Product ID

      
Every product is assigned a unique internal ID. This ensures that even if two products have the same name, the
        system knows they are different.


      
Duplication

      
You can get copy of a existing product to make new product or different pricing. This is useful when adding
        similar items where only the price or size changes. "(Image are duplicated from main once)"


      
Unsaved Changes

      
If you start editing a product or invoice and try to go back without saving, the app will trigger a popup
        warning to prevent accidental data loss. "(Working when you Enable it from Settings)"


      
System Loading

      
When you first open the app, it will load all your products and data from the local database. This can take a few seconds, but it's important for the app to function properly.
        Price Lister allow you to add infinite products, so it's important to have a fast loading system.
