Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Price Lister, we value your privacy above all else. This Privacy Policy outlines how our application handles your data. Our core principle is simple: Your data belongs to you.

1. Data Collection

Price Lister is a 100% offline application. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal information, product data, or usage statistics with external servers.

2. Permissions

To provide full functionality, the app requires specific permissions on your device:

  • Camera: Required only for the Barcode Scanner feature to scan UPC/EAN codes.
  • Storage (Read/Write): Required to save your database, export invoices (PDF/Excel), and create local backup files.

These permissions are used strictly locally on your device.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data created within the app (products, categories, prices, logs) is stored in a local database on your smartphone's internal memory.

Since we do not host any servers, you are responsible for keeping your device secure. We highly recommend using the app's Backup Feature regularly to prevent data loss in case of device failure.

4. Third-Party Services

Price Lister contains no advertisements, no analytics trackers (like Google Analytics), and no third-party SDKs that harvest user data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

Email: support@pricelister.app
